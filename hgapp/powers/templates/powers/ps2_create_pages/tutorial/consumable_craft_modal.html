Consumables are single-use items that are easy to share and trade.

Any Contractor who takes a crafting Gift can craft. Each Downtime between Contracts, Contractors can craft one of each of their Consumables for free. Investing a Gift Credit or Improvement into a Consumable Gift grants an extra one for free. Additional Consumables may be crafted at a cost of 1 Exp each.

Crafted Consumables Include:

  • Potions
  • Specialty ammunition
  • Settable traps
  • Deployable super-tech
  • Magic makeup
  • Energy drinks that turn you into a mongoose
If it's supernatural, single-use items your Contractor makes, it's Consumable Crafting.
