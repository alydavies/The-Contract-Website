Crafting

A Crafting Location is a specially-prepared location where your crafting takes place, such as your home, laboratory, or wizard’s tower. If you do not have access to your Crafting Location (for example, if it is destroyed or you are imprisoned), you cannot craft. If you have the freedom and materials, you may spend a Downtime creating a Crafting Location instead of Crafting. Each Contractor receives a single free Crafting Location with their first Artifact Crafting or Consumable Crafting Gift.

Consumable Crafting allows you to create powerful single-use items. Each time you Craft, you may create one of each Consumable you’re capable of crafting for free. If you invested a Reward into a Consumable Crafting Gift this Downtime, you may create an additional one of that Consumable for free. You may craft additional Consumables at a cost of 1 Exp each.

Artifact Crafting imbues a supernatural effect into a new or existing Crafted Artifact. Artifact Crafting costs Experience equal to the cost of the Gift plus 1 to perform. Investing a Reward into an Artifact Crafting Gift grants an additional free crafting of that Artifact this Downtime.

After Crafting, the items you've crafted appear in the "Stock" tab of your Contractor's character sheet. There, Artifacts may be renamed and edited, and Consumables and Artifacts may be transferred to other Contractors.

You may edit this Downtime's crafting until your next Contract. Once the Downtime passes, you can no longer refund the items you've crafted.
