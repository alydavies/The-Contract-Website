Most Contractors start as humans who awaken their supernatural potential by claiming victory on Contracts.

Contractors advance quickly. They will grow into any sort of archetype (or creature) you desire after only a few victories.

It is possible to create a Contractor who starts non-human or who has had a premature awakening. However, doing so requires a solid understanding of the setting and the Gift system.

New Players should create Contractors that begin their career as normal humans.
