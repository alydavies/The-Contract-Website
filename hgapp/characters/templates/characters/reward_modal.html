First Four Gifts

This guidance helps your Contractor feel useful in a wide range of situations while remaining flavorful and true to their concept.

  • The "Puzzle Piece" Gift
    • If there is a situation where it would be bizarre if your Contractor failed, this Gift ensures that victory.
      • A chef's Power to make food everyone loves
      • A beastmaster’s ability to control animals
      • A driver’s ability to claim victory in a high-speed chase
  • A Combat Gift
    • This is an Action your Contractor can take in Combat AFTER the fighting has started.
    • Contractors don't have to deal with violence by killing things, but if they don't have some way to deal with someone running at them with a knife, they will end up in the Graveyard.
    • Beware purely passive, defensive Effects that don't provide any options such as Barrier and Armor.
  • An Info-gathering Gift
    • Something that provides unique and useful information.
    • Awareness Effects such as Heightened Senses and Investigative Effects are good choices.
  • A Gift that Breaks Rules
    • These Gifts help you create opportunities by breaking the "rules" standard humans abide by.
    • Examples include mobility Gifts that allow reaching new area or influence Powers to control an interaction such as Disguise or Emotion Control.
