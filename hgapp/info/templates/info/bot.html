The House is a dice-rolling bot that makes rolling dice and calculating your Outcome easy as possible. Simply type "!!5" to roll 5 dice at Difficulty 6 and calculate the Outcome, or "!!4@8" to roll 4 dice at Difficulty 8. Typing "!!help" shows a full usage guide.
