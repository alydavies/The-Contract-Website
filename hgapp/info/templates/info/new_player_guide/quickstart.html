Introduction to The Contract

{% cache 5000 quickstart4 %}
This Guide is an introduction to The Contract.
  • It explains the game's core mechanics alongside a short narrative that covers the events of a typical play session.
  • Read this guide front to back without skipping. The narrative contains important information.
  • If you would like a more direct explanation of the game without accompanying narrative or if you want details about any rule introduced in this guide, read the Rules Reference in the Player's Guide.
When you finish this Guide:
  • You will know what The Contract is and how to play it.
  • You will have made your own Contractor.
  • You will either be scheduled to participate in an upcoming online Contract OR you will be prepared to start running The Contract for your own group.
