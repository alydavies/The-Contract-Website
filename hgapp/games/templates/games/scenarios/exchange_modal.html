Support The Contract and unlock new Scenarios

The Scenario Exchange is yet another way for GMs to unlock new Scenarios. These Scenarios cost Exchange Credits, which could be obtained by backing our Kickstarter or donating your own Scenarios to the Exchange. Each Scenario costs 100 Exchange Credits to unlock.

Donating your Scenarios is a great way to support The Contract if you lack the funds to subscribe to our Patreon!

How do I donate a Scenario?

Scenario writers can donate a Scenario to the Exchange via its page in their Scenario Collection. In order to submit, the Scenario must have been run as a Contract, and its writeup must be at least 1000 words long. Next, The Contract's administrators will vet the writeup to ensure it meets our quality standards and may approve it or ask for revisions.

Once your Scenario is approved, it's added to the Scenario Exchange, and you receive 340 Exchange Credits.

A note about spoilers

Contractors and their Players go into each Contract completely blind. Even a Player knowing that a Scenario takes place in the jungle or favors investigation can affect their choice of Contractor or decision to participate. Because of this, this page reveals as little as possible about the nature of each Scenario.
